# 🛒 Retail Sales EDA Project

## 📌 Objective
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to uncover insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed
- Product performance analysis
- Regional sales and returns analysis
- Customer behavior analysis
- Discount and promotion impact
- Delivery and shipping analysis
- Returns analysis
- Payment method analysis
- Time-based (monthly & yearly) trends

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Tablet, Laptop, and Monitor are top revenue-generating products
- North region generates the highest revenue, while South and West show higher return rates
- Wholesale customers purchase more but also have higher returns
- Free shipping is more effective than discount-based promotions
- Discounts do not significantly impact sales volume
- Mid-range shipping costs generate higher revenue than very low or high costs
- Approximately 24.78% of orders are returned
- Sales show strong seasonality with peak performance in Q1

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue
- Improve logistics and product quality in high-return regions
- Optimize wholesale order handling to reduce return rates
- Use free shipping strategically instead of heavy discounts
- Maintain optimal shipping cost ranges
- Encourage digital payment methods
- Implement better return management strategies
- Plan inventory and marketing based on seasonal trends

---

## 📈 Conclusion
This analysis highlights key factors influencing retail sales, including product demand, regional performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. The findings suggest that strategic focus on high-performing products, optimized shipping and promotion strategies, and improved return management can significantly enhance overall business performance.
